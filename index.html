Urbana's Historic Resources Web Map


Welcome to the City of Urbana's Historic Resources web map! This interactive map provides information and photos for all of Urbana's local and national historic landmarks, and for each of “Urbana's 100 Most Important Buildings”. The “100 most” list was created in 2012 and was based on nominations from residents who felt that each building was worthy of recognition.


So please, browse around the map! You're sure to learn a little bit more about many of Urbana's most important and memorable historic buildings. If you have suggestions for improving the map or would like to share information or photos about a specific property, feel free to contact us at kjgarcia@urbanaillinois.us.

Thanks for visiting!
